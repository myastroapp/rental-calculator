How the numbers are calculated

NOI = effective rent (after vacancy) − operating expenses (taxes, insurance, maintenance, management, HOA, other), excluding the mortgage. Cap rate = NOI ÷ purchase price. Cash-on-cash = annual cash flow (NOI − mortgage payments) ÷ total cash invested (down payment + closing + rehab). Estimates only — verify with your lender and a local agent.
