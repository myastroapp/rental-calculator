Privacy Policy — Rental Property Calculator

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This calculator runs entirely in your browser. The numbers you enter are used only to compute results on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. The figures you type are never sent to or stored on any server.

Payments

If you unlock the full report, payment is handled by Stripe; we never see or store your card details. The unlock itself is stored locally in your browser.
