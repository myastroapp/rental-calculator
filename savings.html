How it works

The calculator grows any money you've already saved at your expected return, then solves for the fixed monthly contribution whose compounded growth covers the rest of the goal by your deadline. A higher expected return means less you need to contribute (compounding does more of the work); a shorter timeline means more. Returns aren't guaranteed — for a cash savings goal, use a conservative or 0% rate.
